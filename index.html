What is Yearli?

Yearli is an iOS app that shows your entire year in one view. Connect Apple Calendar and Google Calendar to see all your events at a glance—plan and review your schedule without the clutter.

Features

Year view

A single, customizable view of your calendar across the full year.

Apple & Google Calendar

Works with Apple Calendar and Google Calendar in one place.

Filter & choose

Pick which calendars to show and filter events your way.

Multiple views

Switch between different views for work, personal, or custom setups.

Your data stays yours

If you connect Google Calendar, Yearli asks for read-only access (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly). We use it only to show your events in the app and to power features like filtering and different views. Your calendar data is processed on your device and is not stored or shared by us.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
